EXPERIENCE

GIRLS WHO CODE

Over summer 2015, I participated in the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion program at Google in San Francisco. The program was Monday through Friday for seven weeks, at least 38 hours each week. I received intensive instruction in computer science and programming. Some of the skills I learned include the programming languages of Python, Javascript, and jQuery, along with HTML and CSS. In addition, we learned about robotics, graphics and animation, mobile applications, and data structures. The curriculum was also accompanied by various speakers, demonstrations, workshops, and field trips to technology companies. At the end of the program, we created final projects, where we applied the skills we learned. For my project, I developed a volunteer website that recommends service opportunities in the Bay Area based on one's interests and location. Overall, I found this experience very rewarding because I expanded my knowledge of computer science.
